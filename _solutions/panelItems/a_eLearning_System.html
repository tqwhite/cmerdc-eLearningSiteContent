An eLearning Portal is a variety of eLearning solutions working together to create a seamless integrated solution for your staff and students.

You can have a website, learning management system, content management system and e-portfolio all in one integrated place. When your student logs into one place they are automatically logged into all areas, so they can access information and resources easier. Try our e-portal today to see just how easy eLearning management can be.

With an eLearning Portal you can:

  • Design an online Presence
  • Differentiate/ Individualize Learning
  • Organize Resources
  • Showcase ideas
  • Build communities of learners
