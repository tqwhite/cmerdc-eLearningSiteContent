Powerful Learning Platform

offers teacher and student tools to activate learning. Teachers can personalize activities for each student easily. Learning outcomes can be tied to activities to keep everyone informed on progress toward learning goals.

Key Components


  • Variety of activities.
  • Ability to use exisiting resources.
  • Differentiate content for each student.
  • Record and keep track of learning goals and outcomes.
  • At-risk management tools to keep students focused.
  • Integration with a variety of services.
