6th Grade Math Teacher

Ms. Smith is a 6th grade math teacher at a mid-sized school district in Minnesota. She attended a Flipped Classroom seminar during her summer break and is interested in implementing this teaching method in her class. After some investigation, she came up with a list of essential technologies needed for this to be implemented.
What she needs (Tools that can help):
  • An online communication portal that doubles as a place to put resources for students to use at home or some other designated time. (Moodle)
  • A place where she can put a variety of activities for group work to differentiate for learners (Moodle)
  • Live and asynchronous video tools (BigBlueButton Lecture Capture and Video Hosting)
  • Software to create video lectures. (Smart Notebook Recording)
  • E-portfolios to store students evidence of success (Mahara)
