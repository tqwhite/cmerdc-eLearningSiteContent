Time

It takes time to evaluate programs, implement systems, integrate software, train staff, train students, train parents, build buy-in, build online curriculum, monitor, revise, view reports, and analyze success.

Most online or hybrid programs that fail to flourish, fall victim to one or more in the list above.

So you may be asking yourself, how can we successful implement and maintain an online/hybrid program in our school district? Or saying, I've already tried this and it didn't work.

While a piece of software may help you get started, your success is going to depend upon training, support, and a community of other users to help you along the way.
